Speaker: Rachel Research

Higher-dimensional symmetry of AdS2xS2 correlators

Abstract: It was recently shown that IIB supergravity on AdS5xS5 enjoys 10d conformal symmetry and that superstring theory on this background can be described using a 10d scalar effective field theory. In this paper we adapt these two complementary approaches to correlators of hypermultiplets in AdS2xS2.

Speaker: Patrick Physics

Internal boundaries of the loop amplituhedron

Abstract: The strict definition of positive geometry implies that all maximal residues of its canonical form are ±1. We observe, however, that the loop integrand of the amplitude in planar N=4 super Yang-Mills has maximal residues not equal to ±1.

Thursday 12/09:

Discussion chair: Tina Theory

Exploring Reggeon bound states in strongly-coupled N = 4 super Yang-Mills

Abstract: In this note, we show that the set of possible excitations appearing in any analytic continuation in the multi-Regge limit for any number of particles is rather constrained. In particular, we show that the BFKL eigenvalue of any possible Reggeon bound state is a multiple of the two-Reggeon BFKL eigenvalue appearing in the six-gluon case.

Speaker: Amir Academia

Recursion relations for anomalous dimensions in the 6d (2,0) theory

Abstract: We derive recursion relations for the anomalous dimensions of double-trace operators occurring in the conformal block expansion of four-point stress tensor correlators in the 6d (2, 0) theory, which encode higher-derivative corrections to supergravity in AdS_7xS^4 arising from M-theory.

Thursday 19/09:

Discussion chair: Professor Proton

Multi-Regge Limit of the n-Gluon Bubble Ansatz

Abstract: We investigate n-gluon scattering amplitudes in the multi-Regge region of supersymmetric Yang-Mills theory at strong coupling. Through a careful analysis of the thermodynamic bubble ansatz (TBA) for surfaces in AdS (5) with n-g(lu)on boundary conditions we demonstrate that the multi-Regge limit probes the large volume regime of the TBA.
